Joori Suh Ph.D., NCIDQ, LEED AP joori.suh@uc.edu Joori Suh is an Assistant Professor of the School of Architecture and Interior Design at the University of Cincinnati. As an educator and researcher, she explores the interdisciplinary study of design, theory, and technology that may leverage the current understanding of human creativity and people's interaction with the environment. She is the developer of IGATY software and is hoping to expand the software to make it a rich resource for ideation and exploration.

Morgan Denes is the programmer of IGATY. He is a technical writer by profession but enjoys programming. He enjoys learning new things and was happy to work on the IGATY project.
